How to make a SANDWICH

  1. Select bread, meat, and veggies
  2. Slice veggies
  3. Lie each slice of bread on plate
  4. Use knife to spread condiments on each slice of bread
  5. Add meat and veggies to one slice of bread on top of condiments side
  6. Place remaining slice of bread on top of slice with meat with the condiments facing the meat
